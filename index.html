Feed the HUNGRY

“A smile in a poor man’s face reflects the glory of God”

The purpose to feed the hunger is to say in action that there is somebody there to love them and care them. In our city chennai we witness 15000 homeless people and we have now started to feed 200 people every day in and around koyembedu and Vadapalani in Chennai. Our future vision is to feed in all 15 municipalities in chennai by opening outlets in 15 areas.

Help us feed them

Mr. Arockiaraj & Mrs. Hema Arockiaraj

This noble work was started by Mr. Arockiaraj and Mrs. Hema Arockiaraj. They are already in this field of healing the wounds of this society for more than 23 years. They are residing in chennai and they are doing this social work as a team. The team is comprised of individuals, families from various stream of life. They live in chennai and are blessed with three children. They are simple and have willingness to partner with any sect of people and work for the upliftment of the society.

What we are doing?

Check out the great services which we are doing

Free distribution of food for homeless

Today we identified 200 homeless people lying down in the street, met them, spoke to them kindly, fed them and made them understand that we are with them. This is our day to day doing. Our future hope and aim is to reach all the 300 pincodes of the city to locate the hunger, satify them and enable them to understand their inner ability to give a NEW MEANING to their life.


Everyday we are feeding the homeless people at two points. First meeting point is outside Metro station in Vadapalani junction, and the Second meeting point is outside CMDA bus stop in koyembedu. In these two meeting point we queue up the homeless people and distribute foods.

Caring Covid Affected Patients

We have now given access to the voluntary workers to collect food packets from these two meeting points to give food to the covid affected patients held up at home.

Free Provisions

Free provision bags are given to widowed families and families below the poverty line. Hundreds are being benefitted. This bag contains all the basic provisions to feed 2 people daily for about a month.
